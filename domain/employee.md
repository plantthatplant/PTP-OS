# Employee

## Purpose
A person who works in the operation. Employees make **Observations**, take
**Decisions**, and carry out **Tasks**. Modeling them lets PTP OS assign work,
attribute decisions, and reason about capacity.

## Relationships
- Works in one or more **Greenhouses**.
- Author of **Observations** and **Decisions**.
- Assignee of **Tasks**.
- Recipient of **Morning Intelligence**.

## Important properties
- Identity: stable id, name.
- Role / responsibilities.
- Contact and working schedule (for task assignment and capacity).
- Active status.

## Future integrations
- **Fortnox** — authoritative source for HR/payroll data via a provider; PTP OS
  keeps only the operational view it needs.
- **Even G2 glasses** — an employee is the wearer/identity for hands-free use.

## Examples
- "Anna — head grower, full-time, manages Zones A–B."
- "Erik — propagation specialist, part-time Tue/Thu."
