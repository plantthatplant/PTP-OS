# Task

## Purpose
A unit of work to be carried out in the operation, often arising from a
**Decision** or **Recommendation**. Tasks turn intelligence into action and give
Morning Intelligence something concrete to present and track.

## Relationships
- May originate from a **Decision** or **Recommendation**.
- Assigned to an **Employee**.
- Scoped to a subject/location: **Zone**, **Bench**, **Plant Batch**,
  **Mother Plant**, **Order**, etc.
- Completion may generate **Observations** (e.g. "treated, mites reduced").

## Important properties
- Identity: stable id.
- Title/description and subject/location.
- Origin (decision/recommendation reference, if any).
- Assignee, due date, priority.
- Status (open, in progress, done, skipped) and completion notes.

## Future integrations
- **Even G2 glasses** — tasks surfaced and checked off hands-free in the
  greenhouse.
- **Fortnox** — labor time on tasks may inform cost/payroll later.

## Examples
- "Take 60 cuttings from Mother M-MD-01" — assigned to Erik, due today, high
  priority.
- "Apply biological pest control to Batch MD-2026-014, bench B-03" — from a
  decision.
