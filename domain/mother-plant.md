# Mother Plant

## Purpose
A plant deliberately kept as a long-lived source of propagation material
(cuttings). Mother plants are high-value assets: the genetics and health of future
batches depend on them, so they are tracked individually.

## Relationships
- Is a specialized **Plant** of a given **Species**.
- Produces cuttings that start new **Plant Batches**.
- Located on a **Bench** (often in a dedicated **Zone**).
- Subject of **Observations**; target of **Tasks** (feeding, pruning, taking
  cuttings) and **Recommendations**.

## Important properties
- Identity: stable id, name/label.
- Species and known genetics/cultivar.
- Health and vigor status.
- Productivity: cuttings taken over time, last harvest date.
- Age / establishment date.

## Future integrations
- **Cameras** — visual health monitoring of valuable stock.
- **Synopta** — environment tuned specifically for mother-plant health.

## Examples
- "Mother M-MD-01": an established *Monstera deliciosa* stock plant that has
  yielded ~600 cuttings since 2024.
- A newly designated mother plant being grown out before first harvest.
