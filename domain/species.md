# Species

## Purpose
The botanical type of a plant, together with the cultivation characteristics PTP OS
needs to reason about it (ideal climate, growth timelines, common issues). Species
is the knowledge anchor that lets recommendations be biologically grounded.

## Relationships
- Classifies **Plants**, **Plant Batches**, and **Mother Plants**.
- Referenced by the **Decision Library** / Knowledge for species-specific rules.
- Informs **Recommendations** (e.g. target climate per species).

## Important properties
- Identity: stable id, scientific name, common name(s), cultivar where relevant.
- Cultivation profile: ideal temperature/humidity/light ranges, typical
  propagation method, expected time-to-finish.
- Known risks: common pests, diseases, stress signs.
- Commercial info: typical sellable forms/sizes.

## Future integrations
- **Knowledge Platform** — primary home for deepening species knowledge.
- **Shopify** — species maps to product categories/lines.
- **Synopta** — species targets compared against measured climate.

## Examples
- *Monstera deliciosa* — warm, humid, bright indirect light; ~14 weeks
  cutting-to-finish; watch for spider mites.
- *Ficus lyrata* — sensitive to cold drafts and overwatering.
