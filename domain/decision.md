# Decision

## Purpose
What was actually decided and done (or deliberately not done) in response to a
**Recommendation** or a grower's own judgment. Decisions are the record of action.
They are essential to trust and learning: the system can only improve if it knows
what was chosen, by whom, and why.

## Relationships
- Often originates from a **Recommendation** (accepted, modified, or rejected).
- Made by an **Employee** (or the system, once trusted to act).
- About a subject: **Zone**, **Plant Batch**, **Mother Plant**, **Order**, etc.
- May spawn one or more **Tasks**.
- Its outcome is recorded in **Memory**.
- Catalogued by type in the **Decision Library**.

## Important properties
- Identity: stable id, timestamp.
- Source recommendation (if any) and how it was changed.
- Decision maker and subject.
- Chosen action (structured) and rationale/notes.
- Status (planned, executed, cancelled) and outcome link.

## Future integrations
- **Memory Engine** — closes the loop from decision to outcome.
- **Even G2 glasses** — decisions may be confirmed hands-free in the greenhouse.

## Examples
- "Accepted the recommendation to lower Zone A night temp by 2 °C; executed
  2026-06-26 by Anna." → outcome tracked over the next week.
- "Rejected the cutting recommendation — mother plant showing early pest signs."
