## What Gaia is

Gaia is an **AI-native operating system for professional greenhouse production** — a digital
**Head Grower**. Not a dashboard with AI bolted on: a system whose primary mode is intelligence
that observes, reasons, explains, and learns alongside the grower.

Built for **Kålaberga** first — proven in daily real-world use — and, once it has earned trust
there, usable by other professional growers.

## What it is for

- Help growers make **better decisions**.
- Improve **plant quality**.
- Reduce **manual analysis**.
- **Learn continuously** from what actually happens.

## What success is

Not features shipped — **trust earned and decisions improved**, measured in **daily usage**. The
grower opens the day to a calm, prioritised, explained briefing; relies on Gaia the way they would
on an experienced, trusted colleague; and the system gets better because it remembers.

## The shape of the experience

- **The daily companion is the interface** — the day begins with intelligence, not data entry.
- **Lead with answers and reasoning**; raw data is support, not the main event.
- **Calm and honest** — few words, the right ones, with confidence and uncertainty shown.
- **No layer assumes a screen** — the experience should one day meet the grower hands-free, in the
  greenhouse, where the work is.

## The defining constraint

The value we build must **outlive any source of data**. Greenhouse data enters only through the
Provider Layer; swapping one source for another (Synopta, a feed, a manual import) must change
nothing above it. Sensors and vendors come and go; **the plant is the subject.**

## Guiding beliefs

- **Biology before technology** — the plant's reality leads; technology serves it.
- **Trust before automation** — earn the right to act by first being reliably right and transparent.
- **Learning is the product** — a system that improves from outcomes is worth more than any clever
  feature.

## The long arc

A trusted greenhouse intelligence that combines biological knowledge, continuous learning,
operational memory, and honest reasoning to support growers in real time — and that, over years of
completed recommendations, slowly **becomes a better grower** (see
[`docs/gaia-learning-loop.md`](docs/gaia-learning-loop.md)).
