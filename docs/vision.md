# Vision

## What PTP OS is

PTP OS is an **AI-native operating system for professional greenhouse
production**. It is not a dashboard with an AI feature bolted on; it is a system
whose primary mode of interaction is intelligence — observing, reasoning,
explaining, and learning alongside the grower.

The long-term goal is a **digital greenhouse intelligence** that:

- helps growers make better decisions,
- improves plant quality,
- reduces manual analysis, and
- continuously learns from experience.

## Who it is for

PTP OS is built for **Plant That Plant** first. It is designed to become the
digital operating intelligence of our own greenhouse, proven in daily real-world
use. Once it earns trust there, it is intended to be usable by **other
professional growers**.

## What success looks like

Success is not measured in features shipped. It is measured in **trust earned and
decisions improved**:

- A grower starts the day with **Morning Intelligence** and gets a clear, calm
  picture of what matters today — without digging through screens.
- Recommendations come with **reasoning the grower can follow and judge**, not
  opaque outputs.
- The system **remembers** what was decided and what happened, and gets better
  because of it.
- Over time, the grower relies on PTP OS the way they would rely on an
  experienced, trusted colleague.

## The shape of the experience

- **Morning Intelligence is the primary interface.** The day begins with
  intelligence, not with data entry.
- **AI-first workflow, not dashboards.** We lead with answers and reasoning;
  charts and raw data are support, not the main event.
- **Even G2 glasses are a future primary interface.** The experience should
  eventually meet the grower where the work happens — in the greenhouse, hands
  free — so the architecture must not assume a screen.

## How we get there (direction, not schedule)

PTP OS begins with **Claude Dispatch** as the greenhouse data provider so we can
deliver value immediately. Later, **Synopta Pro/API** replaces Claude Dispatch as
the data source **without changing the application architecture**. This is a
deliberate, defining constraint: the value we build on top of greenhouse data
must outlive any particular source of that data.

## Why this matters

Greenhouse production is biological, continuous, and high-stakes. Small,
well-timed decisions compound into plant quality and yield. Much of the relevant
knowledge today lives in people's heads and is lost when attention is elsewhere.
PTP OS exists to **capture that knowledge, apply it consistently, explain it, and
improve it** — so growers spend less time analyzing and more time growing.

## Guiding beliefs

- **Biology before technology.** The plant's reality leads; technology serves it.
- **Trust before automation.** PTP OS earns the right to act by first being
  reliably right and transparent.
- **Learning is the product.** A system that improves from outcomes is worth more
  than any single clever feature.
